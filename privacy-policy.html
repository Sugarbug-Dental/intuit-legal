Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

This privacy policy describes how Sugarbug Dental ("Company") handles data in connection with its internal QuickBooks Online integration tool ("Application").

1. Data Collection

This Application accesses financial transaction data from QuickBooks Online through Intuit's official API. The data accessed includes:

  • Account information (account names and identifiers)
  • Transaction records (deposits, payments, dates, amounts, and descriptions)
  • Company information associated with the connected QuickBooks account

2. Data Use

All data accessed by this Application is used solely for Sugarbug Dental's internal business operations, including financial reporting and transaction management. Data is not used for any other purpose.

3. Data Storage

OAuth authentication tokens are stored locally on the machine running the Application. Transaction data is displayed at runtime and is not persisted to any database or external storage system.

4. Data Sharing

Sugarbug Dental does not sell, share, or disclose any data accessed through this Application to third parties. Data is accessed and used exclusively by authorized personnel within Sugarbug Dental.

5. Third-Party Services

This Application connects to QuickBooks Online via Intuit's API. Use of QuickBooks Online is subject to Intuit's own Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

6. Security

The Application uses OAuth 2.0 for authentication and communicates with QuickBooks Online exclusively over HTTPS. Access tokens are refreshed automatically and stored securely on the local filesystem.

7. Changes to This Policy

Sugarbug Dental reserves the right to update this privacy policy at any time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.

8. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact Sugarbug Dental.
